Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 3, 2025

1. General Information

Breather is a mobile application developed to help users perform breathing exercises and stress control. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Advertising Data (AdMob)

Our application uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Unique device identifier (Advertising ID)
  • Device information (model, operating system, version)
  • Approximate location (country/region, not exact location)
  • Application usage data (which screens you visit, frequency of use)
  • Network information (connection type, provider)

2.2 Application Data

  • Language preferences (Spanish/English)
  • Application settings (exercise preferences)
  • Local usage data (exercise progress, preferences)

2.3 Device Permissions

  • Internet: Required to display advertisements
  • Keep screen awake: For breathing exercises

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Advertising

  • Display relevant and personalized advertisements
  • Measure advertisement effectiveness
  • Prevent advertising fraud

3.2 App Functionality

  • Personalize your user experience
  • Remember your language preferences
  • Maintain your exercise progress

4. Sharing Information

4.1 Google AdMob

We share data with Google AdMob to:

  • Display personalized advertisements
  • Measure advertisement performance
  • Comply with advertising policies

4.2 We Do Not Sell Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes.

5. Storage and Security

  • Local data: Stored on your device
  • Advertising data: Processed by Google AdMob
  • Security: We implement industry-standard security measures

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Data portability

7. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use tracking technologies to:

  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Analyze application usage
  • Improve user experience

8. Data Retention

  • Advertising data: According to Google AdMob policies
  • App data: Maintained while you use the application
  • Deleted data: Permanently removed when uninstalling

9. Minors

Our application is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.
